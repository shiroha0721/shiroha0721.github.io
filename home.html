NANO SUNSCREEN COSMETICS

Our nano sunscreen cosmetics are developed by using nanotechnology, which specially processes the active ingredients in sunscreen cosmetics into extremely small structures at the nano level. Combined with the use of a new nano carrier system, the sunscreen effect is enhanced: Nano titanium dioxide and nano zinc oxide are both N-type semiconductors. When irradiated by ultraviolet light, electrons in the valence band can absorb ultraviolet light and be excited to the conduction band, and at the same time produce electron hole pairs, so they can absorb ultraviolet light.


When ultraviolet light acts on nanoparticles in the medium, electrons in the particles are forced to vibrate, becoming a secondary wave source, scattering ultraviolet light in all directions, thus reducing the intensity of ultraviolet light in the direction of irradiation.After vitamin E is nano sized, the effect of vitamin E acetate on protecting, improving dryness and moisturizing skin against free radical damage caused by light, heat and UV stimulation is further expanded, and it is easier to be absorbed by the skin, thus enhancing the efficacy of the sun protection product
